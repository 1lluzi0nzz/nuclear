Listen to music on your terms

There's no need to be subjected to tracking and profiling just to listen to your favourite artists. Nuclear empowers you to listen to what you want, where you want, and how you want, for free.

Stream from any free source on the internet. Nuclear supports Youtube and Soundcloud out of the box, with a plugin system allowing for easy addition of more.

Nuclear supports scrobbling to last.fm and updating the now playing status.

Nuclear is developed on Github under Affero GPL, with the "GNU/Linux first" mindset. This means it respects your freedom, and we're giving you full access to source code, so you can modify it and contribute to the project.
